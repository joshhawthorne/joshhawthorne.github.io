Podcasts

  • 2 Keto Dudes: All the things Keto!
  • Criminal: Short super interesting episodes.
  • Stuff You Should Know: Classic podcast that covers everything interesting in just the right amount of detail.
  • Serial: Each season is a different true story. Season 1 is about the murder of Hae Min Lee. Season 2 is about Pfc. Bowe Bergdahl going AWOL in Afghanistan.
  • S*Town: From the makers of Serial. This is a true story about John B McLemore. This is one of the most interesting and sad stories I've ever heard.
  • Reply All: A podcast about the internet.
  • Mystery Show: Starlee Kine researches the most random "mysteries" you can imagine. This probably my favorite podcast of all time. Just a heads up it was cancelled after the first season.
  • ID10T with Chris Hardwick: Formerly The Nerdist. Celebrities (usually of the nerd variety) get interviewed. I've never heard people open up in an interview as much as they do on this show.
